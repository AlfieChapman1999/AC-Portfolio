    Hello there, I'm Alfie Chapman, an aspiring software developer hailing from South London, England. My fascination with technology ignited at the tender age of six when my family acquired our first PC. Since then, I've been hooked on coding, with Python and C++ being my initial forays at the age of 15, followed by HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for my recent projects, including this portfolio you're looking at!

    My ultimate aspiration is to join a company in an IT role, where I can merge my coding prowess, networking and cloud skills with my deeply held values. I firmly believe in fostering an environment of growth and self-improvement. Having grappled with mental health challenges in the past and undergone years of therapy, I've learned invaluable lessons beyond what self-learning and coding alone could impart. This journey has instilled in me the importance of supportive work environments.

    I am excited about the prospect of bringing both my technical skills and personal warmth to a role where I can uplift and inspire those around me, making a meaningful difference through my work.
